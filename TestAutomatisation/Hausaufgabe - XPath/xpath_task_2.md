2. ** Auth / Sign In**
* Email Field:
 `//input[@type='email']`
* Password Field:
 `//input[@type='password']`
* Sign In Button: 
`//button[text()='Sign In']`
*Create a new account:
`//a[text()='Create a new account]`
*Go to home:
`//a[text()='Go to Home']`

`//input[@type= 'email']`

`//input[@type='password']`
